Hospitals

Health services in Akkaraipattu, Kalmunai, and Batticaloa regions have been enhanced with the addition of new hospitals. These facilities play a vital role in improving access to healthcare for residents, offering a range of medical services and treatments. With the establishment of these hospitals, community members now have closer access to essential healthcare facilities, reducing the need for long-distance travel and ensuring prompt medical attention when needed. The new hospitals contribute to the overall well-being and health outcomes of the communities they serve, fostering a healthier and more resilient population.

Ahmad Ali Hospital

Ahmad Ali Hospital, situated in Kalmunai, stands as a beacon of healthcare excellence, offering compassionate medical services to the local community. With a commitment to quality care, the hospital provides a comprehensive range of medical treatments and facilities to address various health needs. Staffed by dedicated healthcare professionals, Ahmad Ali Hospital prioritizes patient well-being and strives to deliver personalized care with empathy and expertise. Through its modern infrastructure and advanced medical technologies, the hospital ensures efficient diagnosis, treatment and recovery for patients, fostering a healthier community and enhancing healthcare outcomes in Kalmunai and surrounding areas.

Jeevi Hospital

Jeevi Hospital, located in Batticalo, is a renowned healthcare institution dedicated to serving the community with excellence and compassion. With a focus on patient-centric care, Jeevi Hospital offers a wide range of medical services and treatments to address diverse healthcare needs. The hospital prides itself on its team of skilled healthcare professionals who provide attentive and personalized care to patients. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and advanced medical technology, Jeevi Hospital ensures accurate diagnosis, effective treatment and optimal patient outcomes. Committed to promoting health and wellness, Jeevi Hospital plays a vital role in enhancing healthcare access and quality in Batticalo and its surrounding areas.

Mediland Hospital

Mediland Hospital, situated in Kalmunai, is strategically positioned in front of the Ashraf Memorial Hospital, offering accessible healthcare services to the community. With a commitment to providing high-quality medical care, Mediland Hospital serves as a trusted healthcare provider in the region. The hospital is equipped with modern facilities and staffed by skilled medical professionals dedicated to delivering comprehensive healthcare services. Mediland Hospital caters to various medical needs, ensuring prompt diagnosis, effective treatment, and compassionate patient care. By offering a range of medical specialties and services, Mediland Hospital contributes significantly to promoting health and well-being in Kalmunai and its surrounding areas.

EMS Hospital

EMS Hospital, located in Batticalo, stands as a beacon of healthcare excellence in the region. With a strong commitment to patient-centered care and medical innovation, EMS Hospital provides comprehensive medical services to the community. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by highly skilled healthcare professionals, EMS Hospital offers a wide range of medical specialties and treatments to address diverse healthcare needs. From emergency care to specialized medical interventions, EMS Hospital is dedicated to delivering top-notch healthcare services with compassion and integrity. As a trusted healthcare institution in Batticalo, EMS Hospital plays a vital role in promoting health and well-being while ensuring patient satisfaction and safety.

Genius Hospital

Genius Hospital, nestled in the heart of Akkaraipattu, is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to serving the local community with excellence and compassion. With a focus on patient-centered care and cutting-edge medical technology, Genius Hospital offers a wide spectrum of healthcare services tailored to meet the diverse needs of patients. Staffed by a team of highly skilled medical professionals and equipped with modern facilities, Genius Hospital strives to deliver exceptional medical care across various specialties, including emergency medicine, surgery, pediatrics, obstetrics, and more. Committed to upholding the highest standards of quality and safety, Genius Hospital stands as a beacon of health and healing in Akkaraipattu, providing comprehensive healthcare solutions with warmth, expertise, and dedication.

Doctors
